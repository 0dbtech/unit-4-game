Crypto Miner

You will be given a random cryptocurrency target coin at the start of the game.

You will have four coins below. By clicking on a coin you will add a specific amount of points to your crypto count.

You win the game by matching your crypto count to the crypto target. You lose the game if your crypto count goes above the crypto target.

The value of each coin is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the values of each coin will change.

Happy mining!
